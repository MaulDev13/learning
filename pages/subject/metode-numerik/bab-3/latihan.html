Latihan Soal Metode Jacobi (10 Soal)

Berikut adalah 10 soal Sistem Persamaan Linear (SPL) yang dapat diselesaikan menggunakan metode Jacobi. Gunakan tebakan awal x⁰ = (0, 0, 0) kecuali jika dinyatakan lain.

Soal 1

8x₁ + x₂ − x₃ = 8 2x₁ + x₂ + 9x₃ = 12 x₁ − 7x₂ + 2x₃ = -4

Soal 2

10x₁ − x₂ + 2x₃ = 6 −x₁ + 11x₂ − x₃ = 25 2x₁ − x₂ + 10x₃ = -11

Soal 3

4x₁ + x₂ + x₃ = 7 x₁ + 5x₂ + x₃ = 8 x₁ + x₂ + 6x₃ = 9

Soal 4

12x₁ − x₂ + x₃ = 5 −x₁ + 9x₂ − x₃ = 10 x₁ − x₂ + 8x₃ = 7

Soal 5

5x₁ + 2x₂ + x₃ = 12 2x₁ + 6x₂ + x₃ = 15 x₁ + x₂ + 7x₃ = 20

Soal 6

3x₁ − x₂ + x₃ = 4
x₁ + 4x₂ − x₃ = 5
x₁ − x₂ + 5x₃ = 6

Soal 7

9x₁ + x₂ + x₃ = 20
x₁ + 8x₂ − x₃ = 18
2x₁ − x₂ + 7x₃ = 16

Soal 8

6x₁ + x₂ − x₃ = 10
x₁ + 7x₂ + x₃ = 14
x₁ − x₂ + 6x₃ = 12

Soal 9

11x₁ − x₂ + x₃ = 13
−x₁ + 10x₂ − x₃ = 9
x₁ − x₂ + 9x₃ = 8

Soal 10

7x₁ + 2x₂ + x₃ = 17
2x₁ + 8x₂ + x₃ = 19
x₁ + x₂ + 9x₃ = 21
