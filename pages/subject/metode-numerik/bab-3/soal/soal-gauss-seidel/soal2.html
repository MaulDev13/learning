Penyelesaian Soal 2 (Metode Gauss-Seidel)

Metode Gauss-Seidel menggunakan nilai terbaru dalam setiap iterasi, sehingga biasanya lebih cepat konvergen dibanding Jacobi. Perbedaan utama adalah ketika menghitung \( x_2^{(k+1)} \), nilai \( x_1^{(k+1)} \) yang sudah diperbaharui langsung digunakan, bukan \( x_1^{(k)} \).

Metode: Iterasi Gauss-Seidel

Tebakan awal: \( x^{(0)} = (0, 0, 0) \)

Sistem Persamaan

$$ \begin{cases} 10x_1 - x_2 + 2x_3 = 6 \\ -x_1 + 11x_2 - x_3 = 25 \\ 2x_1 - x_2 + 10x_3 = -11 \end{cases} $$

Bentuk Iterasi Gauss-Seidel

Dari persamaan pertama: \( 10x_1 = 6 + x_2 - 2x_3 \) → \( x_1^{(k+1)} = \frac{6 + x_2^{(k)} - 2x_3^{(k)}}{10} \)

Dari persamaan kedua: \( -x_1 + 11x_2 - x_3 = 25 \) → \( 11x_2 = 25 + x_1 + x_3 \) → \( x_2^{(k+1)} = \frac{25 + x_1^{(k+1)} + x_3^{(k)}}{11} \)

Dari persamaan ketiga: \( 2x_1 - x_2 + 10x_3 = -11 \) → \( 10x_3 = -11 - 2x_1 + x_2 \) → \( x_3^{(k+1)} = \frac{-11 - 2x_1^{(k+1)} + x_2^{(k+1)}}{10} \)

def gauss_seidel_step(x1, x2, x3): x1_new = (6 + x2 - 2*x3) / 10 x2_new = (25 + x1_new + x3) / 11 x3_new = (-11 - 2*x1_new + x2_new) / 10 return (x1_new, x2_new, x3_new)

Iterasi 0

\( x^{(0)} = (0, 0, 0) \)

Iterasi 1

Menghitung \( x_1^{(1)} \):

\( x_1^{(1)} = \frac{6 + x_2^{(0)} - 2x_3^{(0)}}{10} = \frac{6 + 0 - 2(0)}{10} = \frac{6}{10} = 0.6 \)

Menghitung \( x_2^{(1)} \) (menggunakan \( x_1^{(1)} \) yang baru):

\( x_2^{(1)} = \frac{25 + x_1^{(1)} + x_3^{(0)}}{11} = \frac{25 + 0.6 + 0}{11} = \frac{25.6}{11} = 2.3272727272727275 \)

Menghitung \( x_3^{(1)} \) (menggunakan \( x_1^{(1)} \) dan \( x_2^{(1)} \) yang baru):

\( x_3^{(1)} = \frac{-11 - 2x_1^{(1)} + x_2^{(1)}}{10} = \frac{-11 - 2(0.6) + 2.3272727272727275}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(1)} = \frac{-11 - 1.2 + 2.3272727272727275}{10} = \frac{-9.872727272727273}{10} = -0.9872727272727273 \)

\( x^{(1)} = (0.6, 2.3272727272727275, -0.9872727272727273) \)

Iterasi 2

Menghitung \( x_1^{(2)} \):

\( x_1^{(2)} = \frac{6 + x_2^{(1)} - 2x_3^{(1)}}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(2)} = \frac{6 + 2.3272727272727275 - 2(-0.9872727272727273)}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(2)} = \frac{6 + 2.3272727272727275 + 1.9745454545454546}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(2)} = \frac{10.301818181818182}{10} = 1.0301818181818182 \)

Menghitung \( x_2^{(2)} \):

\( x_2^{(2)} = \frac{25 + x_1^{(2)} + x_3^{(1)}}{11} \)

\( x_2^{(2)} = \frac{25 + 1.0301818181818182 + (-0.9872727272727273)}{11} \)

\( x_2^{(2)} = \frac{25.04290909090909}{11} = 2.2766280991735537 \)

Menghitung \( x_3^{(2)} \):

\( x_3^{(2)} = \frac{-11 - 2x_1^{(2)} + x_2^{(2)}}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(2)} = \frac{-11 - 2(1.0301818181818182) + 2.2766280991735537}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(2)} = \frac{-11 - 2.0603636363636364 + 2.2766280991735537}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(2)} = \frac{-10.783735537190083}{10} = -1.0783735537190083 \)

\( x^{(2)} = (1.0301818181818182, 2.2766280991735537, -1.0783735537190083) \)

Iterasi 3

Menghitung \( x_1^{(3)} \):

\( x_1^{(3)} = \frac{6 + x_2^{(2)} - 2x_3^{(2)}}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(3)} = \frac{6 + 2.2766280991735537 - 2(-1.0783735537190083)}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(3)} = \frac{6 + 2.2766280991735537 + 2.1567471074380166}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(3)} = \frac{10.43337520661157}{10} = 1.043337520661157 \)

Menghitung \( x_2^{(3)} \):

\( x_2^{(3)} = \frac{25 + x_1^{(3)} + x_3^{(2)}}{11} \)

\( x_2^{(3)} = \frac{25 + 1.043337520661157 + (-1.0783735537190083)}{11} \)

\( x_2^{(3)} = \frac{24.96496396694215}{11} = 2.2694512697220136 \)

Menghitung \( x_3^{(3)} \):

\( x_3^{(3)} = \frac{-11 - 2x_1^{(3)} + x_2^{(3)}}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(3)} = \frac{-11 - 2(1.043337520661157) + 2.2694512697220136}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(3)} = \frac{-11 - 2.086675041322314 + 2.2694512697220136}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(3)} = \frac{-10.8172237716003}{10} = -1.08172237716003 \)

\( x^{(3)} = (1.043337520661157, 2.2694512697220136, -1.08172237716003) \)

Iterasi 4

Menghitung \( x_1^{(4)} \):

\( x_1^{(4)} = \frac{6 + x_2^{(3)} - 2x_3^{(3)}}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(4)} = \frac{6 + 2.2694512697220136 - 2(-1.08172237716003)}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(4)} = \frac{6 + 2.2694512697220136 + 2.16344475432006}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(4)} = \frac{10.432896024042074}{10} = 1.0432896024042074 \)

Menghitung \( x_2^{(4)} \):

\( x_2^{(4)} = \frac{25 + x_1^{(4)} + x_3^{(3)}}{11} \)

\( x_2^{(4)} = \frac{25 + 1.0432896024042074 + (-1.08172237716003)}{11} \)

\( x_2^{(4)} = \frac{24.961567225244177}{11} = 2.269233384113107 \)

Menghitung \( x_3^{(4)} \):

\( x_3^{(4)} = \frac{-11 - 2x_1^{(4)} + x_2^{(4)}}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(4)} = \frac{-11 - 2(1.0432896024042074) + 2.269233384113107}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(4)} = \frac{-11 - 2.0865792048084148 + 2.269233384113107}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(4)} = \frac{-10.817345820695308}{10} = -1.0817345820695308 \)

\( x^{(4)} = (1.0432896024042074, 2.269233384113107, -1.0817345820695308) \)

Iterasi 5

Menghitung \( x_1^{(5)} \):

\( x_1^{(5)} = \frac{6 + x_2^{(4)} - 2x_3^{(4)}}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(5)} = \frac{6 + 2.269233384113107 - 2(-1.0817345820695308)}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(5)} = \frac{6 + 2.269233384113107 + 2.1634691641390616}{10} \)

\( x_1^{(5)} = \frac{10.432702548252169}{10} = 1.0432702548252169 \)

Menghitung \( x_2^{(5)} \):

\( x_2^{(5)} = \frac{25 + x_1^{(5)} + x_3^{(4)}}{11} \)

\( x_2^{(5)} = \frac{25 + 1.0432702548252169 + (-1.0817345820695308)}{11} \)

\( x_2^{(5)} = \frac{24.961535672755686}{11} = 2.2692305157050624 \)

Menghitung \( x_3^{(5)} \):

\( x_3^{(5)} = \frac{-11 - 2x_1^{(5)} + x_2^{(5)}}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(5)} = \frac{-11 - 2(1.0432702548252169) + 2.2692305157050624}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(5)} = \frac{-11 - 2.0865405096504338 + 2.2692305157050624}{10} \)

\( x_3^{(5)} = \frac{-10.817309993945371}{10} = -1.0817309993945371 \)

\( x^{(5)} = (1.0432702548252169, 2.2692305157050624, -1.0817309993945371) \)

Analisis Konvergensi

Dari tabel iterasi di atas, terlihat bahwa:

1. Nilai \( x_1 \) konvergen ke sekitar 1.043

2. Nilai \( x_2 \) konvergen ke sekitar 2.269

3. Nilai \( x_3 \) konvergen ke sekitar -1.082

Perubahan antar iterasi semakin kecil:

\( \Delta x_1 \) dari iterasi 4 ke 5: 1.04328960 → 1.04327025 (perubahan ≈ \( 1.9 \times 10^{-5} \))

Metode Gauss-Seidel menunjukkan konvergensi yang cepat pada sistem ini.

Syarat Konvergensi (Dominasi Diagonal)

Periksa syarat dominasi diagonal ketat:

Baris 1: \( |10| > |-1| + |2| = 3 \) → terpenuhi

Baris 2: \( |11| > |-1| + |-1| = 2 \) → terpenuhi

Baris 3: \( |10| > |2| + |-1| = 3 \) → terpenuhi

Matriks koefisien memenuhi dominasi diagonal ketat, sehingga metode Gauss-Seidel dijamin konvergen.
